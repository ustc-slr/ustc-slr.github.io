About Our Group


Visual Sign Language Research Group targets on understanding visual sign language via computer vision techniques, leading by Prof. Houqiang Li and Prof. Wengang Zhou, at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). We are working on isolated sign language recognition, continuous sign language recognition and continuous sign language translation. We have published 30+ top-tier journal articles and conference papers, including IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TMM, IEEE TCSVT, CVPR, ICCV, NeurIPS, AAAI, IJCAI, ACM MM, etc.
